Sagittarius

November 22nd - December 21st

The centaur is the symbol for this sign. In mythology, the centaur was half man and half horse. The centaur is shown with a bowand wherever the arrow lands, the centaur follows. Those born under this sign will love travelling, discovering new experiences, meeting new people, and learning new things.

Sagittarians crave the freedom of the open road. They are unhappy when they are tied to a normal routine and can become restless if there is not enough variety in their lives. Because they travel so much and are eager to experience new things, this sign is fun to be around and often have a large group of friends. They question everything and think that everyone should do the same.


Strengths

generous, idealistic, great sense of humor

Weaknesses

promises more than can deliver, very impatient, will say anything no matter how undiplomatic

Colour

purple - the color of spirituality and awareness
