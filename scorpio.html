Scorpio

October 23rd - November 21st

The symbol for Scorpio is the scorpion. A scorpio person is not necessarily aggressive, unless provoked, and in general would rather be contemplative. They enjoy being alone and often become annoyed when they are around people for too long. Scorpio signs are great secret keepers and feel emotions more intensely than other signs. Because of this they are good at helping people with their problems. They are able to show others the best and the worst in their lives.

Scorpio signs are not fearful individuals and often try things that others would not attempt. This is because they become self-aware at an earlier age than most signs. They have an inflamed sense of justice when it comes to standing up for people. Scorpio signs are interested in what makes people tick and often work in fields that allow them to study people.

Strengths

resourceful, brave, passionate, stubborn, a true friend

Weaknesses

distrusting, jealous, secretive, violent
