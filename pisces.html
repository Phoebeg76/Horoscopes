Pisces

Febuary 19th - March 20th

The fish is the symbol given to this sign, because fish live in a secretive and mysterious place. Pisces tend to be daydreamers, often dreaming up brilliant ideas and plans which they cannot make a reality. They have a happy and vibrant inner life that few get to see. They are deeply caring about others and hate to see people become unhappy.

They are dedicated individuals, staying with a job for decades where others move from one job to the next and similarly with relationships.
