Aries

March 21st - April 19th

The symbol for this sign is that of a ram for good reason. Aries possesses high energy, and this sign is always looking for a challenge. A typical Aries is full of vitality, curiosity, has a heightened sense of justice and enjoys competition. Aries is in it to win it and loves to show off. The Aries person is a leader and likes being in control. They are often impatient and abrupt with those leading them whom they feel are not their equals. Most Aries do not like being told what to do if the person in charge is less talented than them.

Aries is a fire sign which means that they tend to have the characteristics of fire: warm, vital, and bright. They can be impulsive and have hot tempers but they are generally optimistic. However, they have a desire to protect and will make amends when they are wrong.

Strengths

courageous, determined, confident, enthusiastic, optimistic, honest, passionate

Weaknesses

impatient, moody, short-tempered, impulsive, aggressive
