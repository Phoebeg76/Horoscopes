Virgo

August 23rd - September 22nd

This sign is cool and calm which is why the symbol of the Maiden was chosen. Virgo people are mild mannered on the surface, but not underneath. Their minds are never quiet; always thinking, calculating, assessing and they love making something out of nothing. They tend to be detail oriented and like producing something that is not only useful, but beautiful and skillful.
