Gemini

May 21st - June 20th

The Gemini symbol is a pair of twins. This is because this sign doesn't like doing anything alone. Gemini are happiest than when they're sharing ideas with someone else. Communication is a key element for this sign, so many of this sign go into communication related work. Curiosity is a key characteristic of this sign, and they love people. Gemini are great at parties because they can talk about anything with anyone. Gemini are adventurous by nature and so like travelling as often as they can as it they like the opportunity to meet new people, experience new ideas, and to learn new concepts.

Gemini are air signs, which means that they have light, airy personalities. This sign craves intellectual stimulation and will push the envelope physically, mentally, and spiritually. Gemini signs are gluttons for new experiences. This sign is also incredibly creative, which makes them great entrepreneurs, designers, writers, speakers, and anything artistic.

Strengths

gentle, affectionate, curious, adaptable, ability to learn quickly and exchange ideas

Weaknesses

nervous, inconsistent, indecisive
